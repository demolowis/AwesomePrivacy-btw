Linux OS

SELECT FULL DISK ENCRYPTION ON THE INSTALLER! Full Disk Encryption is essential for privacy and security. It will help you to keep your data out from praying eyes, especially in the case of thieft, cyber-attack, or from authorities.

Linux Mint

A GNU/Linux distribution which works out of the box. It's a user friendly desktop operative system which, unlike Windows, respects your privacy and won't waste your computer resources.
